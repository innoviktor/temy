AdSense Integration Guide

How to replace placeholder ad spaces with real Google AdSense ads on your HQTechTalk website.

About Advertisement Spaces

Your website now includes strategically placed advertisement spaces ready for AdSense integration. These placeholder areas show exactly where ads will appear and what sizes they support.

Available Ad Spaces

The following ad spaces have been integrated throughout your website:

Top Banner Ads
  • Desktop: 728x90 Leaderboard
  • Mobile: 320x50 Mobile Banner
  • Location: Top of page after header
Sidebar Ads
  • Size: 300x250 Rectangle
  • Position: Sticky sidebar
  • Location: News and deals pages
In-Content Ads
  • Size: 300x250 Rectangle
  • Location: Between content sections
  • Placement: Home, news, and article pages
Bottom Banner Ads
  • Size: 728x90 Leaderboard
  • Location: Above footer on all pages
  • Visibility: Desktop and tablet only

How to Replace with Real AdSense Ads

Prerequisites

You need a Google AdSense account and approved website. Visit adsense.google.com to get started.

Step 1: Create Ad Units in AdSense

  1. Log into your Google AdSense account
  2. Go to "Ads" â†’ "By ad unit"
  3. Click "Create new ad unit"
  4. Create the following ad units:
    • Header Banner: 728x90 Display ad
    • Mobile Banner: 320x50 Display ad
    • Sidebar/Content Rectangle: 300x250 Display ad
  5. Copy the ad code for each unit

Step 2: Replace Placeholder HTML

Replace the placeholder ad HTML with your AdSense code. Here's what to look for and replace:

Current Placeholder Code:
<div class="ad-banner ad-leaderboard">
    <div class="ad-banner-content">
        <div class="ad-label">Advertisement</div>
        <div>728 x 90 Leaderboard Ad Space</div>
    </div>
</div>
Replace With AdSense Code:
<div class="ad-banner ad-leaderboard">
    <script async src="https://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js?client=ca-pub-XXXXXXXXXX"
         crossorigin="anonymous"></script>
    <ins class="adsbygoogle"
         style="display:block"
         data-ad-client="ca-pub-XXXXXXXXXX"
         data-ad-slot="XXXXXXXXXX"
         data-ad-format="auto"
         data-full-width-responsive="true"></ins>
    <script>
         (adsbygoogle = window.adsbygoogle || []).push({});
    </script>
</div>

Step 3: Files to Update

You'll need to update the ad placeholders in these files:

  • index.html - Home page ads
  • news.html - News page ads
  • deals.html - Deals page ads
  • article-*.html - Individual article page ads

Step 4: Add AdSense Header Script

Add the AdSense script to the <head> section of each page:

<script async src="https://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/js/adsbygoogle.js?client=ca-pub-XXXXXXXXXX"
     crossorigin="anonymous"></script>

Ad Performance Tips

Optimization
  • Use responsive ad units
  • Test different ad placements
  • Monitor click-through rates
  • Optimize for mobile users
User Experience
  • Don't overwhelm with too many ads
  • Ensure fast page loading
  • Keep content above the fold
  • Use relevant ad targeting

Alternative Ad Networks

Besides Google AdSense, your ad spaces are compatible with other advertising networks:

  • Media.net - Yahoo/Bing contextual ads
  • Amazon Associates - Affiliate product recommendations
  • PropellerAds - Display and push notification ads
  • Infolinks - In-text advertising
  • Direct sponsors - Custom advertiser partnerships
Ready for Monetization

Your website is now fully prepared for advertisement integration. The professional ad placement and responsive design will help maximize your revenue potential while maintaining a great user experience.
